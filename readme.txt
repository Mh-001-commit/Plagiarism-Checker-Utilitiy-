Welcome to our project "Simple Plagiarism Detector"

This a step by step instruction on how to use the program:

- First choose whether to check for plagiarism or exit (choose 1 or 2).
- Then, you will be asked to enter the number of the orginal sourses (These are like the websites on the internet or the articles).
- After that, type in their names with the right extension.
- Next, you will be asked to type in the name of the document to be checked for plagiarism (This is like the document you upload on a plagiarism detector).
- Finally, choose between the given algorithms to be executed (Naive - KMP - Boyer Moore - Rabin Karp). 
- You will get the results after that.
